Sysadmin

As one of the top high schools in the nation, TJ has many unique opportunities. One of these is being part of the Sysadmins, a team of students who maintain Ion (the school's intranet), Director (a website management application used by students), webmail for students, high performance computing clusters, and more.





During my freshman year when I applied to be a sysadmin, 8 out of 73 applicants were selected. Since then, I have contributed to the team and currently hold the position of Documentation Co-Lead. My responsibility includes maintaining documentation of systems in the lab for future sysadmins to reference. However, I also contribute to the other teams.
