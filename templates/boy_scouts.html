Project Explanation

Hello! My name is Pratyush Saxena, and this is my Eagle Scout Project. I led a team of scouts in conducting interviews with the seniors at Dulles South Senior Citizen Center. There were two objectives of this project; firstly, to give the seniors someone to talk to and contribute to their mental well-being. The second goal was for the younger generations to learn from the senior citizens' advice and life experiences. Overall, conducting this project took 150 man-hours.
